Privacy Policy — Tipping Calculator World

Effective date: 2026-05-04 · Last updated: 2026-05-04 · Developer: Manuel García Heredia · Contact: serviciosde.apps26@gmail.com

Plain-English summary. We do not collect any personal information about you. The app works entirely on your device. The only third-party service we use is Google AdMob, which serves the advertisements that allow us to keep the app free. AdMob may collect technical data (device identifiers, IP address, ad interactions) for advertising purposes; you can opt out via the consent dialog shown on first launch.

1. Information we collect ourselves

None. Tipping Calculator World does not have user accounts, does not require sign-in, and does not collect or transmit any personal identifiers.

The app stores the following data locally on your device in the operating system's standard preferences storage. This data never leaves your device:

  • The country code you last selected.
  • Your list of recently used countries (up to 5).
  • Your theme preference (light/dark/auto).
  • Your language preference.
  • Your rounding preference.
  • A counter of how many calculations you've performed (used to time the rating prompt).
  • A flag indicating whether you've already seen the rating prompt.
  • A timestamp until which ads are removed (if you used the rewarded "ad-free 24h" option).

Uninstalling the app deletes all of this data permanently.

2. Information collected by Google AdMob

The app shows ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID).
  • IP address (used to approximate location at country level for ad relevance).
  • Device type, OS version, language, time zone.
  • Information about your interactions with ads (impressions, clicks).

Google uses this data to:

  • Serve ads (personalized or non-personalized depending on your consent).
  • Measure ad performance and prevent fraud.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Google's full privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time:

  • Within the app: tap the consent dialog shown on first launch (re-trigger via uninstall/reinstall) and choose "non-personalized ads only".
  • In Android system settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID or Opt out of Ads Personalization.

3. Legal basis for processing (GDPR — EU/EEA/UK users)

For users in the European Union, European Economic Area, or United Kingdom:

  • Local data on your device: not subject to GDPR processing — we do not act as a controller of data that never leaves your device.
  • Ads served by Google AdMob: Google acts as a joint controller / processor depending on the ad type. The legal basis is your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), gathered via the Google UMP (User Messaging Platform) consent dialog on first launch.

You have the right to:

  • Withdraw consent at any time via the in-app consent dialog or your device settings.
  • Access, rectify, erase, or port any data Google holds about you. Use Google's tools at myaccount.google.com.
  • Object to processing or request restriction.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

We do not transfer any data ourselves. Google AdMob may transfer data outside the EU/EEA under standard contractual clauses; details in Google's policy.

4. Children's privacy (COPPA — under-13)

This app is not directed at children under 13. The Play Store target audience is set to 13+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will work with Google to address it.

5. Data retention

  • Local app data: retained on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data via device settings.
  • AdMob data: retained according to Google's retention policies. See Google's privacy policy for details.

6. Permissions used

The app requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET — required by AdMob to load ads.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — required by AdMob to detect connectivity.

The app does not request location, contacts, camera, microphone, storage, or any other permission.

7. Third parties

The only third party with whom data is shared (indirectly, via the ad SDK) is Google AdMob / Google LLC.

We do not use:

  • Analytics SDKs (Firebase Analytics, Amplitude, Mixpanel, etc.) — V1 has no analytics.
  • Crash reporting SDKs.
  • Social media SDKs.
  • Push notification services.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the app changes. Material changes will be communicated via an update note on the Play Store listing. The "Last updated" date at the top of this document reflects the most recent revision.
